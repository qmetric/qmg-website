How does QMetric use cookies?

If you visit the QMetric website, we collect information about your online preferences in a “cookie” (a small, unique identifier text file) which is stored on your computer or mobile phone. QMetric uses cookies for a number of purposes e.g. to personalise content or to monitor traffic on our website.

When visiting our website you may see some cookies created that are not related to QMetric. This is because some features on our website use services provided by other companies’ websites. Those companies control and are responsible for the creation of those cookies.

Most browsers allow you to set your cookie preferences so that you can decide how you would like to interact with our website: whether to be notified interactively about cookies or to accept or reject all cookies. Since each browser is different you should check the Help menu in your browser to determine how to do this.

Do I need to enable cookies to use the QMetric website?

You don't need to allow your browser to accept cookies in order to view much of our website but if you don't accept them, some parts of the QMetric website may not function properly or in some cases may not operate at all.
